Welcome into the future of quality. This inaugural session marks the official go-live of **QualiView Lab** and brings together QA professionals, product teams, engineering leads, and organisations interested in building and sustaining quality in their software.

**Quality Assurance in Motion** is a monthly community series focused on real-world software quality and testing practices across different types of organisations and teams.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Friday, 1 May 2026
- **Time:** 19:00 – 19:40 (Johannesburg time)
- **Format:** Virtual (Zoom)

---

## Session Focus

### Part 1: Introducing QualiView Lab

An in-depth introduction to QualiView Lab, who we are, why we exist, and how we support teams and organisations in building reliable, high-quality software.

### Part 2: Open Discussion on Quality in Practice

A facilitated, open conversation around what effective QA looks like in different environments, the value of manual testing, and how quality evolves as teams and products grow.
